About Sveinn

Hi, I’m Sveinn! I'm a queer illustrator, born and raised in Iceland. I am currently working, living, and studying, at Arts University Bournemouth, UK, with my partner of 2 years.

Since the age of 7, I have had a stammer which created many challenges, such as bullying, growing up. From this experience I have become an active member of the Icelandic Stuttering association and I hope my work will help bring visibility to those who stammer.

I have also become incredibly aware of the the issues of representation surrounding queer culture regarding race, gender stereotypes, and body image. My artwork and practice touches upon many of these issues in a unique way, often laced with a little humor.
